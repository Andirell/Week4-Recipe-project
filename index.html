Ingredients

For Chicken:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 3 eggs whisked
  • 1/4 cuo cornstarch (for chicken)
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • salt and pepper to add flavor
  • oil of choice (for frying)

For Sweet and Sour Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoon of vinegar
  • 1/4 cup catsup
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ginger powder
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of sesame seeds
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • sliced green onion

How to cook?

  • Cut chicken into bite-size pieces.
  • Mix cornstarch, all purpose flour and whisked eggs and dredge the chicken.
  • Fry the chicken until golden brown.
  • To make the sauce - mix honey, say sauce, vinegar, catsup, ginger, garlic, salt, pepper and chili flakes. Heat in a pan for 5minutes.
  • To thicken the sauce, whisk cornstarch with 2 tablespoons of water until you form a paste. Pour this into the sweet and sour sauce and continue whisking until the sauce thickens.
  • Toss the chicken with the sweet and sour sauce until fully coated.
  • Serve with rice and garnish with sesame seeds and green onion.
